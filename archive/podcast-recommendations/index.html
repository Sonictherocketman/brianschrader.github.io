Podcast Recommendations

Posted on Tue, 21 Oct 2014 at 12:00 AM

I saw a few other people posting podcast recommendations and I thought they were lacking a few crucial entries. Here's my list:

  • Analog(ue) with Myke Hurley and Casey Liss

  • Astronomy 162 - Stars, Galaxies, & the Universe with Professor Richard Pogge *

  • ATP with Marco Arment, John Siracusa, and Casey Liss *

  • Back to Work with Merlin Mann and Dan Benjamin

  • Connected with Myke Hurley, Stephen Hackett, and Federico Viticci

  • Core Intuition with Daniel Jalkut and Manton Reece

  • Debug with Guy English and Rene Ritchie

  • Edge Cases with Andrew Pontious and Wolf Rentzsch *

  • Hardcore History with Dan Carlin

  • Hello Internet with CGP Grey and Brady Haran

  • Internet History Podcast with Brian McCullough

  • Melton with Don Melton and Rene Ritchie

  • Pragmatic with John Chidgey

  • Roderick on the Line with John Roderick and Merlin Mann *

  • The Talk Show with John Gruber

  • * Highly recommended

I listen to a lot of podcasts...
