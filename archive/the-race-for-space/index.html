The Race for Space

Posted on Sun, 06 Sep 2015 at 10:22 PM

I've been looping this album for the last 3 days. If you like space and the Apollo missions, then you need to listen to Race for Space the debut album from Public Service Broadcasting (spotify link).

The entire album is a collection of radio transmissions from the Apollo missions put to music. Yeah, cool right?
